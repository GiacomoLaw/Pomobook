Graph power-up

12/11/2016

0 Comments

 
Add a graph to see your Pomobook productivity trend! The graph is simple. Simply follow these steps:
  1. Look at your total Pomodoro's used everyday. 
  2. Create a graph for every month, with the x-axis being your days, and the y-axis being the amount of Pomodoro's you have used.
  3. Everyday, log the amount of Pomodoro's you have used.
  4. Eventually, you will get a graph showing you your productivity 'trend'.
A completed month on a Pomobook graph
A simple and cool way to keep track of your productivity trend!

    What are power-ups?

    Power-ups are small things that you can add to your Pomobook, that can make it more powerful and offer more things.

    However, these things are just optional. You don't need them!
