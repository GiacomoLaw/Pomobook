Pomobook - A simple analog pomodoro tracking technique
Pomobook a simple analog way of managing tasks that is integrated with the Pomodoro technique. It allows you to see your trends of productivity, as well as keep track of your Pomodoro's and tasks, all in one place. It's loosely based on the well known Bulletjournal, however with one key difference; it's specifically designed with the Pomodoro Technique in mind.

Pomobook can be added to any form of paper you have, from a Moleskine to a pukka pad. It's completely free, simple to setup and easy to use. Don't like the way it's set up? Customize it to your hearts content.
